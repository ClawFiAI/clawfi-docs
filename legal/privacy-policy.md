# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

ClawFi ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension, SDK, API, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address and authentication credentials when you create an API account
- **API Keys**: Generated keys for accessing our services
- **Support Communications**: Information you provide when contacting support

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

#### Browser Extension
- **Blockchain Addresses**: Token and wallet addresses you view on supported sites (processed locally, not transmitted)
- **Extension Settings**: Your preferences and configuration (stored locally in browser)
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous feature usage statistics (can be disabled in settings)

#### SDK & API
- **Request Metadata**: IP address, request timestamps, and endpoint usage for rate limiting
- **Error Logs**: Anonymous error reports for service improvement

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Private Keys**: We never collect, store, or have access to your wallet private keys
- **Seed Phrases**: We never collect or request your recovery phrases
- **Transaction Signing**: We cannot sign transactions on your behalf
- **Browsing History**: We do not track your general browsing activity
- **Personal Financial Data**: We do not access your actual wallet balances or holdings

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- **Provide Services**: Deliver token risk analysis and market data
- **Improve Services**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
- **Communication**: Send service updates and respond to inquiries
- **Compliance**: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms

## 3. Data Processing Location

### 3.1 Local Processing (Extension)

The ClawFi browser extension performs **all token analysis locally** in your browser:

- Risk calculations happen on your device
- Token data is fetched directly from public blockchain APIs
- No analyzed data is sent to ClawFi servers unless you explicitly enable cloud features

### 3.2 Server Processing (API/SDK)

When using our API or SDK with cloud features:

- Requests are processed on secure servers
- Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Servers are located in the United States and European Union

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### 4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these circumstances:

| Recipient | Purpose | Data Shared |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **Service Providers** | Infrastructure and hosting | Anonymized usage metrics |
| **Legal Authorities** | Legal compliance | Only when legally required |
| **Business Transfers** | Merger or acquisition | With user notification |

### 4.3 Public Blockchain Data

Our Services analyze publicly available blockchain data. Any information you submit to a public blockchain is inherently public and not covered by this policy.
