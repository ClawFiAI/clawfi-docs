## Do Your Own Research (DYOR)

ClawFi should be **one tool among many** in your research process:

### Recommended Due Diligence

- [ ] Read the project's whitepaper
- [ ] Review smart contract audits
- [ ] Check team backgrounds and doxxing
- [ ] Analyze tokenomics and vesting
- [ ] Monitor social sentiment
- [ ] Verify liquidity depth
- [ ] Test with small amounts first
- [ ] Set stop-losses and risk limits

### Red Flags to Watch

- Anonymous teams with no track record
- Unrealistic promises or guarantees
- Pressure to invest quickly
- Locked or hidden contract functions
- Concentrated token holdings
- Fake social media engagement
- Copied or plagiarized code
